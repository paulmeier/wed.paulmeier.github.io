Questions?

Hopefully we can answer them here.

Q: What will I wear?

Dress comfortably for a September outdoor ceremony.


Q: Is there a shuttle from the resort to Devil's Lake?

No. We encourage guests to carpool.


Q: Are kids allowed?

Yes, the park is a great place for kids!


Q: Is the ceremony inside or outside?

The ceremony will take place inside the Red Oak Building.


Q: Is it a buffet or plated meal?

Dinner will be served buffet style with a variety of different options. There is no seperate kids menu but we'll have plenty of options for everyone.


Q: What happens if I RSVP yes but then something changes and I can't make it?

Just send the RSVP form on this website again with the option selected 'Not Attending.'


Q: What is the parking situation?

We will be providing you a prepaid parking pass via mail a few weeks prior to the wedding. That said, we encourage guests to car pool from the hotel since lines into the park can be long and parking limited. We will have the Red Oak building open all day for those who wish to arrive early to enjoy the park before the ceremony. Please note the ceremony takes place on the South Shore of the lake and the reception is a short distance away on the North Shore. Guests should plan on driving from the ceremony to the reception, and the parking pass is valid in both locaitons. Please see the maps section and itenerary for further details.


Q: Will alcohol be provided?

Beer and wine will be provided free of charge. Hard alcohol is not allowed inside the reception building (Chateau.)


Q: Are the parking passes for all day?

Yes! Do not be alarmed that it says it's a 1 hour pass. These passes are good for the entire day.
