Directions

Getting to and around Devil's Lake can be challenging. Please print off or save maps before entering the park as there is limited cell phone service. Below we've tried to provide as much information to get you safely in and out!

Getting to Devil's Lake from Chicago

For those of you coming from Chicago there are a few different ways. I suggest following route 12 to get into the park. For those of you who would like to be adventerous and take a ferry you can go through Merrimac.
